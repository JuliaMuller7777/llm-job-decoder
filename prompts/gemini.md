# Gemini (2.5) — Job Description Decoder

Gemini is multimodal, so you don't have to copy text. Screenshot the LinkedIn /
careers-page posting and upload the image — Gemini reads it directly.

Upload the screenshot (or paste the text), then send the prompt below.

---

```
You are a former B2B hiring manager and recruiter. Read the job description in the
image (or text) I've given you for its subtext, not its surface. Be blunt and
specific. Do not flatter me.

If anything in the image is cut off or unreadable, say so before decoding.

Return these sections:

1. TL;DR — in two sentences, what is the team actually missing and what will this
   person really do day to day?
2. Seniority reality check — title inflated, deflated, or honest? Quote the phrases
   that prove it.
3. Comp & leverage signals — where is there room to negotiate?
4. Red flags — scope creep, "wear many hats," reorg, backfill churn, vague metrics.
   Quote the phrase, then translate it.
5. Position yourself — the 3 things to lead with to get screened IN.
6. Questions to ask — 5 sharp ones that signal seniority.
7. New headcount or backfill? — your best read and why.

Be concrete. Quote the posting. No filler.
```
